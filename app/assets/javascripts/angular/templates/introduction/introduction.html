Introduction |

This tool is a Title 24 Compliance Tool with Simplified Geometry.

You can travel back and forth in this navigation and continue editing your model. Save your model and return to your account page later to finish it.

Project: In this section you will add the project location and members of the project team.

Building: This section allows you to describe the total square footage of your building and add stories to the building.

Constructions: Define some default constructions for your building. These will be applied to the spaces as you make them, but you can edit and refine them at an individual surface level.

Spaces: The first time you enter the spaces section on a project you will get a dialog that will help you quickly set up your basic spaces. Then you can refine and edit the spaces in a table view. In this section you will set constructions, space functions, lighting, loads, and other settings for each space.

Thermal Zones: Create thermal zones and assign spaces to those thermal zones in this section.

Systems: Add HVAC systems and plants to your building, refine their settings, and attach them to thermal zones. A limited number of systems are provided.

Review and Edit: This section provides you with some feedback on fields you may have missed that are required and links back to those sections to allow you to complete your compliance check.

Check Compliance: Run your compliance check and get a PDF Report.
